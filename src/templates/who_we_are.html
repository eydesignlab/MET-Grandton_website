who we are

Grandton Police Service, commonly referred to as GPS, is the largest police force in the country, with 49,600 staff, comprising of 31,000 sworn police officers, 8,900 police staff and 1,600 non-sworn police community support officers (PCSO’s). GPS serves a 9.1 million population across 30 boroughs.
