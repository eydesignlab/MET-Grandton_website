REPORT AN INCIDENT

If you've witnessed or been the victim of crime, please refer to the options below.

REPORT A CRIME

You can report a crime to us via your local police station, or by phone or email. If you feel you are in danger or the situation is an emergency, please dial 999.

ANTISOCIAL BEHAVIOUR

Does it feel like the situation could get heated or violent very soon? Is someone in immediate danger? Do you need support right away? If so, please call 999 now.

MISSING PERSON

If the missing person is in immediate danger, is a young child or vulnerable to harm, call 999 now.

ROAD TRAFFIC INCIDENTS

If a crime is happening now or someone is in immediate danger, call 999.

LOST OR STOLEN VEHICLES

Unless you have reason to believe your car has been stolen, please call TRACE on 0300 123 2040.

LOST OR STOLEN PROPERTY

If you've lost or found property in a public place, we may be able to help. Contact us via your nearest police station and we'll reply to you within 24 hours with an update.
