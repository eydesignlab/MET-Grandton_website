ENVIRONMENTAL

At Inala, we make every effort to cut down on waste and environmental degradation. Why? Because, as avid outdoor activists, we like to wear our own TrailStep shoes out on the beautiful trails of this planet! We have implemented the following environmentally based initiatives to help keep the planet beautiful:

Implemented a Lean Manufacturing process to reduce waste of materials and energy

Use water-based adhesives in our shoes to cut back on emissions of harmful chemicals

Use recycled polyester in our cloth uppers

Use cloth made from recycled Tbottles in some of our apparel

Seek sources of Torganic cotton

Use rubber from sustainablymanaged rubber tree farms

Social

At Inala, we believe in social equity and justice. Toward that end, we maintain the following standards:

  • We are an equal opportunity employer. We pay a fair and competitive wage in every locality.
  • We maintain safe and healthy workplaces and encourage our employees to maintain a work-life balance.
  • We inspect the facilities of our sub-contractors to ensure that they maintain the same level of health and safety at their facilities that we expect in our own facilities.
  • Our sponsored athletes deliver lectures across the United States, introducing people to the beauty of nature and health-giving benefits of running or maintaining an active lifestyle.
